Frequently Asked Questions

You can edit any record within 24 hours of entry. Simply go to 'Records', find the transaction, and click 'Edit'. For older records, please contact your administrator.
Go to 'Reports' section, select the month you want, then click the 'Print' button at the top-right corner. Make sure your printer is connected before printing.
Only authorized Mothers' Union officials (Chairlady, Secretary, Treasurer) can access financial data. All data is encrypted and securely stored.
Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your email. Contact admin if you don't receive the reset email.
